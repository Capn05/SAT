Student Review

100% worth it

I bought this service right after I took the March Digital SAT and immediately realized how helpful it was. I got a 1300 on the March SAT and knew I needed more than one month. So, I upgraded from the one month plan to the three month plan and grinded hundreds of questions over the summer getting my accuracy up. The best part of the program is definitely the individual questions and how they have them mapped out in the subsections that College Board has laid out on their SAT practice question bank. The AI tool giving hints is also helpful and is good at giving step by steps to help solve difficult questions.

The only minor concern I have is that some of the practice tests do have questions that are extremely difficult, especially in module 1 which doesn't exactly mimic the real exam but this might not even be a flaw. Preparing with these harder practice tests(harder than bluebook practice tests) was super helpful on the real test and I got a 1510 on the October Exam. I'd recommend this for anyone who needs help getting those hard module 2 questions down and anyone who wants to learn the fundamental questions of the exam.
